# Originality and Copyright

## Non-Negotiable Rule

Aldrym may be heavily inspired by the feel and gameplay loop of classic Tibia-like MMORPGs, but all concrete creative content must be original.

## Do Not Copy

- Sprites, icons, UI art, tilesets, maps, or sound assets
- NPC names, city names, monster names, item names, quest names, spell names, lore, or dialogue
- Client or server files from Tibia, CipSoft products, OpenTibia distributions, or third-party derivatives
- Protected formulas, data packs, or proprietary content bundles

## Practical Guidance

- Build original lore, geography, terminology, visuals, quests, and progression content
- Use references for mood and structure, not for direct reproduction
- Prefer original naming early to avoid accidental placeholder drift into copied material
